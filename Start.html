Monero is a secure, confidential, digital currency. It doesn't matter what you're doing - Monero keeps you safe.

How does Monero work?

Monero uses a blockchain and neat cryptographic proofs to ensure that you can transact privately, and miners to verify nobody is cheating the network. Monero uses a layer of privacy protocols, ensuring the receipient, sender, and amount are all hidden. Click here to learn more about the Protocol
