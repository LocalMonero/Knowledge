Getting a Wallet

Wallets are important; they're what make Cryptocurrencies decentralized. It means you can control the mathematical keys that ensure only you have access to your funds, and that nobody else can stop you from using them.

The first thing to do is find a wallet that suites your needs - and there's tons to select from! Are you interested in completely maximizing your privacy, or is having private transactions enough for you? Are you interested in having an easy setup, or would you rather have full control over everything?

The one thing to remember about any wallet is to write down the mnemonic seed it gives you. This will allow you to always access your funds!
