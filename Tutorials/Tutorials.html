Accepting Monero
Accepting Monero can be done a variety of different ways, depending on your needs. Do you want to immediately settle for Fiat, or keep your XMR? Do you want a simple website plugin, or do you want full control over the implementation?

The options are vast, but the important thing to remember is how you want it to look in the end.

Spending Monero
Want to spend Monero privately, and support stores that accept it too? The first thing you need to do is download a wallet (or don't, there's webwallets). When choosing a wallet, there's a few things you want to keep in mind. Are you interested in completely maximizing your privacy, or are having private transactions enough? Are you interested in having an easy setup, or would you rather have full control over everything?

The one thing to remember about any wallet is to write down the mnemonic seed it gives you. Keeping this safe will ensure you have full access to your funds if your harddrive fails, or if your house ever burns down!
